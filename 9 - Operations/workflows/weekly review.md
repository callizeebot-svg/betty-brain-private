# Weekly Review

## Purpose

Look back across the past week, identify meaningful patterns, and decide what should change in Betty Brain.

## Goals

- review progress and drift
- surface repeated issues
- notice emerging priorities
- identify stale assumptions
- strengthen project and area memory

## Inputs

- recent live logs
- assistant actions
- project notes
- operations notes
- incident or healthcheck notes
- active priorities

## Output

A weekly review note should summarize:

- what happened
- what changed
- what is still unresolved
- what should be updated in the brain
- what deserves focus next week

## Steps

1. Scan the last week's meaningful entries
2. Group developments by project, area, and operational theme
3. Identify patterns, drift, or recurring blockers
4. Update or suggest updates to durable pages
5. Record recommended next actions or attention areas

## Rules

- do not just restate daily logs verbatim
- synthesize patterns and implications
- preserve evidence for meaningful claims
- use the review to improve the brain, not just describe it

## Recommended cadence

- weekly

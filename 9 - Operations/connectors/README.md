\# Connectors



This folder documents external connectors, MCPs, integrations, and linked systems.



\## Purpose

Help Betty Brain understand:

\- what integrations exist

\- where they are configured

\- whether they are working

\- whether they are shared, local-only, or agent-specific

\- what security or auth boundaries apply



\## Connector categories

\- Claude-only MCPs

\- OpenClaw integrations

\- shared external services

\- local machine integrations

\- research and deployment tools



\## Rules

\- do not put secrets in these files

\- document paths, purposes, and status

\- mark broken integrations clearly

\- mark whether an integration is portable, shared, or tool-specific
